Privacy Policy

At Tab Collection Saver, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data and what information we collect:

  • We do not collect or transmit any personal data.
  • All tab collections are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not track or monitor your browsing habits.
  • We require only the minimum permissions necessary for the extension to function properly.

Data Storage

Tab Collection Saver does not store any of your information in external servers. Your data remains securely stored within your device, and no third parties can access it.

Permissions

The permissions we request are solely to enable saving and restoring tabs and collections. We do not use these permissions for any other purpose.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us at support@tabcollectionsaver.com.
