About Tab Collection Saver

Tab Collection Saver is a Chrome extension designed to help you manage your browsing sessions efficiently. Save groups of tabs, restore them later, and never lose your important research or work again.

Features

Save Tab Collections

Save your current tabs as a named collection with a single click.

Restore Collections

Easily restore saved tab collections whenever you need them.

Export/Import

Export your collections for backup or import them on another device.

Privacy Focused

Your data stays on your device, ensuring your browsing habits remain private.

How to Use

  1. Install the Tab Collection Saver extension from the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Click on the extension icon in your browser toolbar to open the popup.
  3. Enter a name for your collection and click "Save Tabs" to save your current tabs.
  4. To restore a collection, simply click on its name in the list of saved collections.
  5. Use the export and import features to backup or transfer your collections.

Privacy Policy

At Tab Collection Saver, we take your privacy seriously. Our extension:

  • Does not collect or transmit any personal data
  • Stores all tab collections locally on your device
  • Does not track your browsing history or habits
  • Requires only necessary permissions to function
